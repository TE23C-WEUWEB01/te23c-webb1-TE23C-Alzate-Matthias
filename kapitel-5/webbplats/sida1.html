Creepy Nuts is a Japanese hip hop duo consisting of DJ Matsunaga and R-Shitei. They made their major label debut in 2017.

The duo performed the opening and ending themes for various anime and live-action television series like Call of the Night, Extremely Inappropriate!, and Dandadan. They also made the theme song of the Spring Koshien broadcasts in 2022.

Their single "Bling-Bang-Bang-Born" was released on January 7, 2024, as the opening theme for the second season of the anime series Mashle: Magic and Muscles. It topped the Billboard Japan Hot 100 for eight consecutive weeks, becoming the duo's first number-one single on the chart as lead artists. It was also a worldwide hit, reaching number eight on the Billboard Global 200.
